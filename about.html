Many Rivers, One Body

Adi Marie is a multi-generational descendant of Mesoamerican, Caribbean, West African, Celtic, and Roma lineages — a convergence of traditions whose wisdom currents run through both her practice and her personhood.

The ancestors speak in her work. Their medicines, their prayers, their ways of seeing the living world inform every session, every divination, every breath.
