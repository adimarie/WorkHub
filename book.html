All work begins with a conversation.

The first step in working together is a brief introductory call — a fifteen-minute conversation where we meet each other, you share what you are carrying, and together we discern what form of support best serves your season of life. The full intake does not happen at booking; it happens in this first call, before any session begins.

All work at The Animist Apothecary is held in sacred reciprocity. This means that exchange between practitioner and receiver is understood as relational, not merely transactional. A sliding scale is offered across all offerings. No one is turned away for financial reasons — if you are navigating genuine hardship, reach out before booking. The conversation itself is part of the care.

To schedule, choose your offering below and write to Adi Marie at the address below. You will receive a response within two to three business days to confirm your date and time.

On Exchange

Sacred Reciprocity

A sliding scale offered across all services — because access to healing should not be determined by financial position alone.

Tier One

Sustaining

The standard rate that sustains the practice and honors the full value of the work. This is the rate for those who are financially stable and employed, for whom this investment is within reach without hardship.

Tier Two

Supporting

Above the standard rate. Choosing this tier is a direct act of solidarity — it is what makes the Supported tier possible for others. For those with financial abundance who wish to share it in this way. This is the circle completing itself.

Tier Three

Supported

A reduced rate available by request, for those experiencing genuine financial hardship. No one is turned away for lack of funds. To inquire about the Supported tier, write to Adi Marie before booking.
