Ongoing Container

Developmental Arcs

Sustained passages of becoming — held across months, shaped to your arc.

A Developmental Arc is not a series of appointments. It is a sustained passage of devoted accompaniment — a living container held across months, shaped by what you are moving through and who you are becoming. It is for those who know, in their bones, that they are in the midst of something larger than any single session can hold.

These are the long crossings: a major life threshold that refuses to be rushed, a grief that has roots older than this lifetime, a spiritual emergence that needs careful tending, an ancestral healing that calls for patience, ceremony, and steady witness. Some passages do not resolve — they reveal. And what they reveal requires time, relationship, and a container built to hold the full arc of your becoming.

Each Developmental Arc is woven from the full range of this work: somatic bodywork and ceremonial touch, divination and ancestral inquiry, ceremony at the seasonal turns, plant medicine and herbal support, integration conversation and spiritual counsel. What arrives in any given session is shaped by what is alive in you at that moment. The work adapts as you change — because real healing is not linear, and a container worthy of your life must be able to move with you.

The long arc of healing is not about fixing what is broken. It is about becoming who you were always trying to be — with enough support that you do not have to become them alone.

The relationship at the center of this work is not incidental — it is the medicine itself. Over time, patterns surface that could not be seen in a single encounter. The body learns to trust. The ancestors draw closer. The soul's longing becomes audible. Adi Marie holds this container with full presence, adapting her methods and her listening to what your particular arc requires, without agenda and without rushing you toward any predetermined outcome.

A Developmental Arc is an act of trust in both directions — yours and hers. The arc belongs to you. The holding belongs to both of you together.

The Intake Process

Every Developmental Arc begins with a two-hour intake session. This is not an assessment — it is a first listening. Together, you will map the landscape of what you are carrying, where you have come from, and what this passage seems to be asking of you. From that conversation, Adi Marie will offer a proposed shape for the container: rhythm of sessions, likely modalities, and what to watch for as the arc unfolds. Nothing is fixed. Everything begins here.

Duration Ongoing — typically 3 to 12 months. Session frequency shaped to your arc and capacity.
Scale Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale. No one is turned away for financial reasons.
