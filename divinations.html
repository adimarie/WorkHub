Oracular Mediumship

The Call of the Unseen

Divination is an ancestral procedural spiritual technology — a living practice of working in direct relationship with spirits, elementals, ancestors, and the intelligent forces woven throughout nature. It is both mystical and utterly practical: spiritual engineering in the service of a life fully lived.

As an initiated Dagara diviner carrying the Crocodile Clan lineage through the Somé tradition, I hold this work with rigor and reverence. What passes between us in a divination session comes not from me, but through me — from the council of forces assembled on your behalf.

Divination reconnects us to severed lines — to the ancestral intelligence that has always known the way through.

What Is Divination?

In the Dagara tradition, divination is a structured conversation with the spirit world — not fortune-telling, but a form of spiritual counsel rooted in thousands of years of accumulated wisdom. The diviner acts as translator between your living questions and the answers that already exist in the unseen.

For many people in the modern West, this lineage of direct spiritual communication has been severed — through colonization, forced displacement, and the slow forgetting that comes with generations of spiritual amnesia. Divination is one way that thread can be picked back up.

Why Now?

You come to divination when something in you knows — before your mind does — that it is time for a different quality of guidance. When the rational mind has exhausted its options. When you are at a threshold. When grief has no name. When a decision carries weight you cannot hold alone.

The spirits are not waiting for you to be ready. They are waiting for you to ask.

The Structure

The Three Steps of Divination

I

The Question

We begin by gathering the emotional and spiritual energies around what troubles or questions you carry into the session. The quality of your question shapes the quality of what is revealed. Together, we refine the thread the spirits will pull.

II

The Divination

Using a diviner's stick and a kit containing stones, shells, bones, and sacred objects, I receive direct answers, wisdom, and ritual prescriptions on your behalf. The session is recorded so you can return to it. Nothing is softened or withheld.

III

The Ritual Path

The divination prescribes offerings and ceremonies to establish ongoing dialogue with the spirit world. These are not symbolic gestures — they are real agreements, real relationships. The work continues after our session ends.
