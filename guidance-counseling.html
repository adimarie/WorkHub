Plàticas

Guidance & Counsel

Spiritual mentorship and life navigation rooted in animist wisdom.

The Offering

For Those at the Crossroads

Some moments in life call for more than touch or technique. They call for a witness — someone who can sit with you at the edge of what you know, help you read the terrain, and offer the kind of honest, loving presence that sees the whole of you.

Plàtica is a word from the Mesoamerican tradition: a sacred conversation, a heart-to-heart. This is the spirit that animates these sessions.

These sessions are for those who are integrating ceremony or plant medicine work, navigating a major life initiation, standing at a crossroads, or seeking counsel rooted in animist wisdom — in eco immersions, plant medicines, somatic guidance, and instruction.

What a Plàtica session is — the format, how it differs from therapy, the animist orientation you bring, the kinds of questions and situations you hold space for. In your own words.
Who benefits most, how long sessions are, what you offer — the depth, the limits, the relational quality of the work.
