A Metamorphosis Has Been Underway

Bodywork & Botanicals
becomes The Animist Apothecary

After 16 years of service as a Somatic Touch Therapist and Hands-on Spiritual Practitioner, this work has entered a new evolution — one shaped by initiation, lineage, and the steady call of the unseen.

What began as massage therapy revealed itself to be something larger: clients would arrive for bodywork carrying wounds that needed spiritual tending, or seeking spiritual guidance while their bodies cried out for touch. The two could not be separated.

And so a new form emerged — one that honors the whole of what a human being is.

What has always been here remains: deep somatic tending, sacred space, relational care, and the devoted art of helping people return to themselves.

Who This Work Is For

You are welcome here

This work does not require you to be well. It requires only that you come as you are, willing to be met.

Trauma survivors pursuing safety, restoration, and agency

Grief carriers and those in mourning for the earth
