A Metamorphosis Has Been Underway

Bodywork & Botanicals becomes
The Animist Apothecary

After sixteen years of service as a somatic touch therapist and hands-on spiritual practitioner, this work has entered a new evolution — one shaped by initiation, lineage, and the steady call of the unseen.

What began as massage therapy revealed itself to be something larger. Clients would arrive for bodywork carrying wounds that needed spiritual tending, or seeking spiritual guidance while their bodies cried out for touch. The two could not be separated. The flesh and the sacred are not divided. They never were.

And so a new form emerged — one that honors the whole of what a human being is: a creature of earth and cosmos, of ancestry and becoming, of body and spirit woven into one living cloth.

The Animist Apothecary is the name this work has grown into. The medicine has not changed. Only the threshold has widened.

What has always been here remains: deep somatic tending, sacred space, relational care, and the devoted art of helping people return to themselves.
