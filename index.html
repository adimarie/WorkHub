A Metamorphosis Has Been Underway

Bodywork & Botanicals becomes The Animist Apothecary

After sixteen years of service as a Somatic Touch Therapist and Hands-on Spiritual Practitioner, my work through Bodywork & Botanicals has come into a new evolution of specialized offerings and curation. What was once a singular path of provision is now interwoven as a supplemental addendum into much deeper transformational offerings of spiritual awakening and healing.

The many people who came seeking respite on the massage table almost always had deeper wounds that needed attending to — expressing in physical symptoms of disharmony within their system.

After over a decade of devoted study, initiation, and maturation as an answer to the deeper calling of my gifts in service to the world, I now have a richer set of specialized skills to provide the much-needed deep work to bring about life-changing contact with the inner domains of Spirit and Self.

Bodywork & Botanicals has become the next evolution of shamanic care:
The Animist Apothecary.

What has always been here remains: deep somatic tending, sacred space, relational care, and the devoted art of helping people return to themselves.
