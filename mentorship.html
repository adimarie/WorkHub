Mentorship Containers

Sustained, progressive pathways of healing — for those ready to go all the way in.

The Container

An Arc of Becoming

Healing that matters rarely happens in a single session. It unfolds — over months, across seasons, through the unglamorous territory of practice and integration and the slow reordering of a life.

Mentorship containers are for those who are ready to commit to that arc. Not because the work is hard, but because they know themselves well enough to know they need sustained companionship, accountability, and depth.

These are developmental containers — offered in three tiers, shaped to where you are and what you are building toward.

What a Developmental Arc container looks like — duration, rhythm of sessions, what's included (bodywork, guidance, ceremony, divination, integration support), who it's designed for, what distinguishes each tier. In your own words.
