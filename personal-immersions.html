Private Ceremony

Personal Immersions & Luminous Breath

A private ceremonial day crafted entirely for you — in your particular season of need.

Some moments in a life demand more than an hour. There are seasons when the ordinary pace of healing — session by session, week by week — cannot keep up with the velocity of what is moving through you. A Personal Immersion is an answer to that call: a full or half-day private ceremonial experience held entirely in service of your particular threshold, your particular soul.

An immersion is not a collection of techniques offered in sequence. It is a single uninterrupted passage of devoted attention — a ceremonial day that breathes and shifts according to what arises. What gets woven in is always responsive to you: shamanic bodywork and ceremonial touch, conscious-connected breathwork, divination to illuminate what the unseen world is communicating, herbal medicine and plant allies, ceremony at whatever threshold you are crossing. The day is yours. The shape emerges from your need.

The Luminous Breath is conscious-connected breathwork held in ceremonial container — a practice of breathing life force deliberately and continuously through the body, clearing the nervous system, dissolving the contractions of old story, and opening the expanded awareness that lives just beneath our ordinary constriction. Through sustained rhythmic breathing, the body becomes a vessel for states of consciousness that cannot be reached by thinking alone: grief releases, the body's intelligence speaks, what has been held for years can finally move. Luminous Breath is available as a standalone session or woven into a full immersion when it is what the day is calling for.

Full Immersion vs. Luminous Breath Standalone

Full Immersion (3–6 hours): A dedicated ceremonial day customized entirely to your threshold. May include shamanic bodywork, Luminous Breath, divination, ceremony, herbal support, plant medicine, integration, and witnessing — shaped entirely by what you are carrying and what this season is asking of you. Held at The Haven in Topanga Canyon.

Luminous Breath Standalone (90 minutes): A focused ceremonial breathwork session held in sacred space. Ideal for those called specifically to this practice, those in integration after other work, or those meeting conscious-connected breathwork for the first time. Available as a single session or as part of an ongoing series.

Both the immersion and the standalone breathwork session begin with a conversation and close with integration time — because what opens during this work deserves care in the landing. You will not be rushed from the table into traffic. The pace of the day, like the work itself, honors what the body and soul need in order to truly arrive.

Duration 3–6 hours for a full immersion; 90 minutes for standalone Luminous Breath.
Scale Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale. No one is turned away for financial reasons.
