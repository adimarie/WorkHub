The Work

Offerings

Each offering is a doorway. Some you enter once; some you walk through again and again as the spiral deepens.

Offering One

Developmental Arcs

A Developmental Arc is not a series of sessions. It is a sustained passage. Over the course of months, we enter into a living container together — shaped around the specific arc of your becoming, the wound you are ready to cross, the form of life that is trying to emerge through you.

This is for those who know they are in the midst of something larger than a single session can hold: a major life threshold, a deep layer of ancestral healing, a creative or spiritual emergence that demands devoted accompaniment. Here, the work is not episodic. It is continuous and cumulative — tended across time the way a living field is tended.

Together we weave somatic bodywork, ceremony, divination, integration, and witnessing into a coherent path — one that adapts as you change, that holds both the tenderness of your wound and the enormity of your becoming.

Duration: Ongoing — typically three to twelve months. Session frequency shaped to your arc and capacity.
Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale. No one is turned away for financial reasons.

Offering Two

Shamanic Bodywork

This is touch as sacred technology. Shamanic bodywork arises at the intersection of somatic therapeutics and non-ordinary awareness — the practitioner moving not only through the physical structures of muscle, fascia, and nerve, but through the more subtle architectures of the energy body, the ancestral field, and the luminous web that underlies all form.

Sessions weave trauma-informed hands-on work with shamanic presence, spiritual discernment, and ceremonial intention. What needs to release in the body is met in the body. What needs witnessing is witnessed. What is ready to return, returns. The session moves between the visible and invisible without forcing a separation between them.

After sixteen years of somatic practice, this work is neither esoteric nor abstract — it is profoundly physical, profoundly present, and profoundly attentive to you.

Duration: 90 minutes. Available as a single session or series.
Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale.

Offering Three

Personal Immersions
& Luminous Breath

A Personal Immersion is a private ceremonial experience crafted entirely around you — your particular season of need, the specific transmission most ready to move through, the threshold your life is standing at. These are full or half-days held in sacred space at The Haven, weaving whichever modalities your moment calls for: breathwork, somatic work, ceremony, divination, herbalism, or some unexpected combination that arises organically in the space between us.

The Luminous Breath is the breathwork thread within this practice — a full-spectrum, conscious-connected breath journey designed to move life force through the body, clear stagnant patterns from the cellular and nervous system, and open access to expanded states of awareness. It is the breath as a portal: not a technique imposed on the body, but the body's own intelligence invited to lead.

The Luminous Breath can be woven into a larger immersion or offered as a standalone session.

Duration: Three to six hours for a full immersion; 90 minutes for a standalone Luminous Breath session.
Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale.

Offering Four

Ceremonial & Ritual Groups

The field that opens in ceremony is amplified and transformed by the presence of community. Group work, in the animist understanding, is not simply efficient — it is necessary. Some healings require witnesses. Some prayers need a chorus. Some thresholds can only be crossed together.

These gatherings are seasonal, thematic, and custom-designed — held at The Haven in Topanga Canyon or in land-based settings where the earth itself becomes the altar. New moon circles, grief rituals, ancestral healing ceremonies, initiation rites, plant medicine circles, and elemental earth ceremonies are among what has been held.

Upcoming gatherings are announced through the newsletter and on Instagram. Custom group containers — for private communities, families, or organizations — are available by inquiry.

Duration and pricing: Vary by gathering and container design.
Community accessibility is central. Sliding scale always available.

Offering Five

Platicas — Guidance & Counsel

Platica is a Spanish word for conversation — but in the curandera tradition, these are not ordinary conversations. They are held in ceremony, in the presence of the unseen, under the watch of ancestors and guides. They are a form of spiritual counsel, life navigation, and mythopoetic companionship for those who are at crossroads, integrating ceremony, or navigating a major initiation.

Adi Marie brings together sixteen years of therapeutic practice, animist wisdom, and direct ancestral guidance into these sessions. You are not receiving advice — you are receiving accompaniment within the deeper intelligence of your own life. The session creates space for what you already know to be heard, held, and spoken back to you in the language of your own soul.

These sessions are also offered to those navigating grief, creative emergence, relational complexity, or the question of what comes next.

Duration: 60 to 90 minutes. Available as a single session or ongoing series.
Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale.

Offering Six

Ancestral & Elemental Divinations

Through the Dagara stick divination tradition of the Wedeme and Kontomble lineages — carried through the Somé family of Burkina Faso — direct communication is established with the forces of the unseen world: your ancestors, your elemental guardians, and the spirit allies who are specifically oriented to your path.

A divination is not fortune-telling. It is a transmission from the realm of the more-than-human — a listening rooted in the intelligence of the earth and the ancestral web. What comes through is often precisely what has been needed: naming, direction, ceremony, reminder, and medicine. It arrives with the weight of something that has been waiting a long time to be heard.

Adi Marie trained directly under Dagara elders Ivan Chocron, Yvonne, and Chad Charles. Her practice is held with deep reverence for this tradition's sacred protocols and the living accountability of its lineage holders.

Duration: 90 minutes.
Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale.
