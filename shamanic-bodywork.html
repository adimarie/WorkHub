Shamanic Bodywork

Touch as sacred technology — tending body and soul simultaneously.

The body is not separate from the soul. It is the soul's home in this world — the place where memory lives when the mind has moved on, where unfinished grief settles into muscle and bone, where the ancestors leave their marks and the spirit's longing makes itself known through pain, through constriction, through the particular way you brace against your own aliveness. Shamanic Bodywork begins with this understanding and does not depart from it.

This work lives at the intersection of somatic therapeutics and non-ordinary awareness. Hands move through the physical body with care and precision — releasing tissue, working with breath, following sensation — while simultaneously tracking the invisible: what is moving in the energetic field, what ancestral or spiritual presence is nearby, what the body is trying to complete that it has not yet been permitted to finish. A session moves between these registers fluidly, guided by what presents itself in each moment.

The body is not an obstacle to spiritual work. It is the most honest oracle available — it has never learned to lie.

Adi Marie's approach is trauma-informed in the fullest sense: not merely cautious with difficult material, but attentive to the nervous system's intelligence, its protective genius, and its capacity for genuine release when met with safety rather than force. Sessions are paced by what the body can integrate, not by what the practitioner intends to accomplish. The body sets the pace. The body holds the wisdom. This work follows.

The spiritual discernment component of each session is not added on — it is woven throughout. Adi Marie works in relationship with helping spirits and her own training in animist tradition to track what is present in the field around the client, naming what is ready to be named and supporting the release or return of what has been held. This is not performance. It is listening at a level most of us were never taught was possible.

Shamanic Bodywork is appropriate for those carrying unresolved trauma held in the body, those navigating spiritual emergence or energetic disruption, those who have felt that ordinary therapeutic work has not been able to reach what lives deepest, and those who are simply ready to be met — in their bodies, fully, without reduction.

Duration 90 minutes. Available as a single session or as an ongoing series.
Scale Offered on Sacred Reciprocity sliding scale. No one is turned away for financial reasons.
